1. Introduction

AI Image Caption Generator ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • API Keys: When you provide API keys for AI services, they are stored locally on your device and encrypted.
  • Settings: Your preferences and configuration settings are stored locally using Chrome's storage API.

2.2 Usage Information

  • Caption History: Generated captions are stored locally on your device (if enabled in settings).
  • Image URLs: Temporarily processed to generate captions, not permanently stored.
  • Anonymous Analytics: Basic usage statistics (only if you opt-in).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Generating AI-powered image captions
  • Saving your preferences and settings
  • Providing caption history functionality
  • Improving the extension (with your consent)
  • Technical support and troubleshooting

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

  • All personal data is stored locally on your device
  • API keys are encrypted before storage
  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers

4.2 Third-Party APIs

  • Images are sent to your chosen AI service (Hugging Face, Google Vision, etc.)
  • We do not store or access images sent to third-party services
  • Third-party services have their own privacy policies

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Third-Party APIs: Images are sent to your selected AI service for captioning
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights
  • Anonymous Data: Aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics (with your consent)

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Data Control: You can delete all stored data through the extension settings
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics and history saving at any time
  • Export: Export your data in CSV or JSON format
  • GDPR Compliance: Enable enhanced privacy protections for EU users

7. Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. International Data Transfers

When using third-party AI services, your data may be processed in different countries. Please review the privacy policies of your chosen AI service providers.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the extension and updating the "Effective Date."

10. Third-Party Services

This extension integrates with third-party AI services. Their privacy policies apply to data processed by them:

  • Hugging Face Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy
